Our Troubleshooting & Diagnostics Services

When production stops, every minute counts. Our industrial troubleshooting team delivers fast, accurate root cause analysis for VFDs, servo drives, PCBs, and complex motion control systems — with emergency on-site response available 24/7 across Houston.

VFD Drive Repair

Diagnosis and repair of variable frequency drives from all major manufacturers. Resolve overcurrent faults, communication errors, and power electronics failures. On-site and bench repair for all VFD brands.

Servo Drive Diagnostics

Root cause analysis for servo drive faults, encoder errors, and motion control issues. We go beyond fault codes to identify the underlying failure mode and prevent recurrence.

Industrial PCB Repair

Component-level repair of industrial printed circuit boards, power supplies, and control cards. We restore boards other shops consider unrepairable, saving you the cost of full replacement modules.

Motion Control Systems

Troubleshooting of multi-axis motion control systems and CNC interpolation issues. Systematic analysis of the full motion chain — controller to drive to motor — to pinpoint failures fast.

On-Site Emergency Service

Rapid field response for critical equipment failures across Houston manufacturing plants. 24/7 availability. Technicians arrive with diagnostic equipment and common spare parts.

Remote Diagnostics

Remote troubleshooting via network access or phone support to accelerate diagnosis and reduce downtime before dispatch. Many faults can be identified and corrective action started immediately.

How We Work

Diagnosis to Root Cause in 3 Steps

1

Call or Submit a Request

Contact us with the equipment type, fault symptoms, and urgency level. We respond within 30 minutes for emergency calls.

2

Remote or On-Site Diagnosis

We begin with remote triage. If on-site is required, our technician arrives with the right equipment to isolate the fault to root cause.

3

Fix & Root Cause Report

We execute the repair and hand you a written root cause report with recommendations to prevent recurrence. 90-day warranty on all repairs.
