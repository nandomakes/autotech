Our Preventive Maintenance Services

Unplanned downtime is the most expensive event in any manufacturing operation. Our preventive maintenance programs catch failures before they happen — keeping your CNC machines, PLCs, drives, and automation systems running reliably, month after month.

CNC Machine Maintenance

Lubrication, filter inspection, parameter backup, axis calibration, and preventive checks to keep CNC machines at peak performance. Visits scheduled to avoid interference with operations.

PLC & Control System Inspection

Battery replacement, firmware updates, I/O testing, and logic backup for Allen-Bradley, Siemens, and Omron PLCs. Protect your programs and keep control systems verified.

Drive & Motor Inspection

Thermal imaging, vibration analysis, and electrical testing of servo drives, VFDs, and motors to detect failures before they occur. Early detection of bearing wear and insulation degradation.

Electrical Cabinet Service

Cleaning, connection torque check, component inspection, and thermal scanning of electrical panels. Loose connections and contamination are leading causes of unexpected failures — we find them first.

Monthly & Quarterly Plans

Custom service agreements tailored to your equipment list and production schedule. Priority scheduling for emergency calls and dedicated technician included.

Documentation & Reporting

Full maintenance logs, equipment health reports, and trend analysis. Clear records of every service visit, finding, and corrective action performed.
