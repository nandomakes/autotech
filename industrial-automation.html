Our Industrial Automation Services

We design and deliver end-to-end automation solutions for Houston manufacturers — from initial PLC programming to full robotics integration and machine vision systems. Every project is engineered to increase throughput, reduce manual labor, and improve process consistency.

PLC Programming

Allen-Bradley, Siemens, Omron, and Mitsubishi PLC programming for machine control, process automation, and system integration. New programs, logic modifications, and legacy system migrations with full documentation.

HMI Development

Custom HMI screens for operator interfaces, process visualization, alarm management, and production monitoring. Intuitive designs that reduce operator error and improve response time.

Robotics Integration

Integration of industrial robots into production lines — programming, tooling, safety systems, and coordination with existing equipment. All major robot brands.

Machine Automation

Upgrade manual or semi-automatic machines to fully automated systems. Sensor integration, actuator control, and closed-loop feedback for consistent, high-speed production.

Machine Vision Systems

Camera-based inspection, part verification, and quality control systems integrated with your production line. Catch defects in real time, reduce scrap, and ensure dimensional compliance.

Industrial Networking

Setup and configuration of EtherNet/IP, Profibus, Modbus, and DeviceNet for connected manufacturing. Reliable, low-latency communication between controllers, drives, and plant systems.

SCADA Systems

SCADA system design and implementation for real-time data monitoring, alarm management, and automated email reporting for data storage and analysis. Full visibility into your plant operations from anywhere.

SCADA in Action

Real-Time Data Monitoring & Email Reporting

We design custom SCADA systems that monitor your plant data in real time and send automated reports via email for record-keeping and analysis. Full visibility into temperature, pressure, production metrics, and equipment status — accessible from anywhere.
