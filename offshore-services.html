Our Offshore Services

We bring our full range of industrial expertise to offshore environments — automation, repair, diagnostics, and maintenance for platforms that can't afford downtime. Every technician is safety-certified and experienced in offshore operations.

Offshore PLC & Automation

PLC programming, HMI development, and control system integration for offshore process equipment. Allen-Bradley, Siemens, and ABB platforms with full offshore safety compliance.

CNC & Rotating Equipment Repair

On-platform repair of CNC machines, servo drives, spindle motors, and rotating equipment. Diagnosis and repair executed on-site to minimize equipment downtime offshore.

Electrical Troubleshooting

Root cause diagnostics for VFDs, motor control centers, power distribution, and instrumentation systems in offshore environments. 24/7 emergency response available.

SCADA & Monitoring Systems

Design, installation, and configuration of SCADA systems for real-time monitoring of offshore operations. Automated reporting and remote data access for onshore teams.

Preventive Maintenance Programs

Scheduled maintenance visits for offshore equipment — electrical inspections, drive maintenance, control system backups, and predictive diagnostics to prevent failures at sea.

Industrial Networking & Comms

Setup and troubleshooting of industrial communication networks on offshore platforms — EtherNet/IP, Modbus, Profibus, and fiber optic backbone systems.
