Our CNC Repair Services

From full machine troubleshooting to servo drive repair and spindle overhaul — certified technicians covering every aspect of CNC repair for Houston manufacturing plants. Backed by 48-hour diagnosis and 90-day warranty.

CNC Machine Troubleshooting

Full fault diagnosis for Fanuc, Mazak, Siemens, Okuma, and Allen-Bradley. Error code analysis, axis faults, and motion control issues identified and resolved with precision.

Servo Drive Repair

Repair of servo amplifiers, drives, and motor feedback systems. Restore precise motion control, eliminate positioning errors, and return your axes to full specification performance.

Spindle Troubleshooting

Diagnosis and repair of spindle motors, bearings, encoder feedback, and spindle drives affecting surface finish and cutting performance. On-site and bench repair available.

Electrical Cabinet Diagnostics

Complete inspection and repair of CNC electrical cabinets: power supplies, relays, wiring, and control boards. We trace faults to the root cause — not just replace parts.

Machine Commissioning & Startup

Post-repair commissioning, parameter backup, axis calibration, and full functional testing before returning equipment to production.
